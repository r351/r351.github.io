Security Is Our Priority

Cold Storage

Our platform keys are generated offline, stored offline, and transactions are signed offline. We drastically reduce cyber security threat by never exposing keys to a network-connected device.

Multi-Signature Process

Our multi-signature process ensures that no single party can move your funds. Key encryption and layers of distribution secure your funds against both internal and external threats.

Safeguards

Our safekeeping framework protects cryptoassets with people, processes, and technology. Get notified prior to fund transfer so you can also review transaction details.
